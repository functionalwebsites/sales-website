Mailto Contact Form Builder

Create a free contact form for a static website. Add text fields, dropdowns, required fields, and a message box, then generate HTML that opens a pre-filled email in the visitor's email app.

Mailto forms are free and static-friendly, but they are not a full form backend. The visitor's email app must open, and the visitor still has to send the email.
