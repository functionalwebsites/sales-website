How to Unlock Premium Items

All custom blocks and custom themes require a Pro plan first. Once Pro is unlocked, items fall into three groups: included advanced items, paid premium add-ons, and custom business builds.

  • Advanced Items: Included with Pro. These are ready to use as soon as your Pro plan is active.
  • Premium Items: Require Pro and a separate one-time purchase for each item or pack.
  • Business Items: Custom solutions for your business. Contact sales for pricing and details.

Start with Pro to unlock custom item support, then add premium blocks or themes only when you need them.
