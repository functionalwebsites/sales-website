Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes. Cancel your subscription anytime with no questions asked. Your sites remain online and you can export them as HTML files.
What happens when I upgrade to Pro? ▼
Your free projects carry over. You immediately get access to the full template library, custom domains, deployment, and analytics. No setup needed.
Can I export my site and host it elsewhere? ▼
Yes. Export your site as a ZIP file containing all HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. You can host it on Netlify, GitHub Pages, AWS S3, or anywhere static files are served.
Do I need technical skills? ▼
No. The builder is designed for non-technical users. Drag, drop, and publish. For advanced users, you can edit the HTML directly.
What's the difference between exporting and deploying? ▼
Export downloads your site as files (Free tier). Deploy publishes your site to the web with a custom domain and HTTPS (Pro tier and up). Deployment is one-click with Netlify or Vercel integration.
Is there a storage limit? ▼
Free tier: 5 projects, each up to 100MB. Pro tier and above: Unlimited storage. Contact support for special cases.
Do you offer bulk discounts? ▼
Yes. If you're building sites for your agency or business, contact us for custom pricing on the Business plan.
What about SEO? ▼
Pro plans and above include SEO tools: meta tag editors, automatic sitemaps, Open Graph tags, and structured data generation. Static sites also load faster, which helps ranking.
